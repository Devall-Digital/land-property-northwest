## 🚀 Quick Deployment

4. Your site will be available at `https://yourusername.github.io/repo-name`

### Option 3: Netlify (Recommended - Free)
1. Visit [Netlify.com](https://netlify.com)
2. Drag and drop all files into Netlify deployment area
3. Your site will be live instantly with HTTPS

## 📊 Analytics Setup

### **Google Analytics 4**
1. Create GA4 property at [analytics.google.com](https://analytics.google.com)
2. Copy your Measurement ID
3. Replace `GA_MEASUREMENT_ID` in `script.js` with your actual ID

### **Google Search Console**
1. Verify your domain at [search.google.com/search-console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Submit your sitemap: `https://yourdomain.com/sitemap.xml`

### **Phone Call Tracking**
The website includes click-to-call functionality. For advanced tracking:
- Consider CallRail or similar services
- Track phone conversions in Google Analytics

### **Colors (CSS Variables)**
